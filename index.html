About The Hackathon

Pythakon'22 is an 18-hour Python Hack-a-Thon designed to allow participants to flex their Python skills and creativity. This hackathon aims to foster a technical temper among the student community and encourage healthy competition. It is an exclusive opportunity for participants to develop and showcase their expertise as well as to create innovative and exciting solutions to real-world problems.
